The Hot Flushes are a Melbourne-based vocal quartet who have been performing together for over 20 years.

Formed in 2002 after a chance meeting in the Sandybeach Choir, the group has grown from humble beginnings at local markets and festivals to regular appearances at prestigious venues such as the MCG and Rippon Lea Estate.

![The Hot Flushes](/images/legacy/casualVIP.JPG)

### Our Story

With a shared love for harmony and performance, The Hot Flushes bring a unique energy to every show. Our repertoire spans from the 30s to the 70s, featuring beloved show tunes and popular classics that resonate with audiences of all ages.
